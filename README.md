## 📖 About

Applian Replay Video Capture 13.0 is a screen recording and video capture utility for Windows and MacOS. It captures streaming video, webinars, gameplay, video calls, and any on-screen activity directly to a local file. No external capture hardware or complex configuration is required — simply launch the executable, select the recording region or window, and start capturing.

## ⚙️ Requirements

- **Operating System**: Windows 10/11 (64-bit) or macOS 10.15+ (Intel/Apple Silicon)
- **Processor**: Intel Core i3 or equivalent (2.0 GHz or higher recommended)
- **RAM**: 4 GB minimum (8 GB recommended)
- **Disk Space**: 200 MB for installation; additional space for recorded video files
- **Graphics**: DirectX 10 compatible GPU or higher
- **Internet**: Required for  activation (one-time) and updates
- **Additional**: Administrator privileges for Windows installation; microphone and speakers for audio capture

## ✨ Features

- **Screen & Window Capture** 🖥️ — Record full screen, a specific window, or a custom region with a single click.
- **Streaming Video ** 🌐 — Capture streaming content from web browsers, media players, and apps (HLS, RTMP, progressive ).
- **Audio Capture** 🎙️ — Record system audio, microphone input, or both simultaneously in sync with video.
- **Output Formats** 📁 — Save recordings as MP4, AVI, WMV, FLV, or MOV with adjustable quality and frame rate (up to 60 FPS).
- **Scheduled Recording** ⏰ — Set a start time and duration for unattended captures — perfect for webinars and live streams.
- **Webcam Overlay** 📷 — Add your webcam feed as a picture-in-picture overlay during recordings.
- **Hotkeys & Cursor Options** ⌨️ — Configure keyboard shortcuts for start/stop/pause, and choose to show or hide the cursor.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Can I capture DRM-protected content?**  
A: No. Replay Video Capture respects digital rights management and will not record protected streams from services like Netflix, Disney+, or Amazon Prime. It is intended for freely available streaming video and local screen activity.

**Q: Why is my audio out of sync with the video?**  
A: This is usually caused by high CPU usage or an unstable frame rate. Reduce the capture resolution or frame rate (e.g., 30 FPS instead of 60), close unnecessary background applications, and ensure you are using a wired internet connection for streaming sources.

**Q: Is there a risk of being banned from a service for using this tool?**  
A: Recording streaming content from websites may violate their terms of service. We recommend checking the terms of each platform and using the software only for personal, educational, or archival purposes. Applian Replay Video Capture is a legitimate screen recording tool — there is no detection risk when recording your own screen activity.
